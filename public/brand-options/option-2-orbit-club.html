Orbit Club

A mission-map learning universe with a bright star pilot, clear routes, and compact badges. It feels energetic while staying structured for parents and kids.

Mascot: Navi Theme: learning missions Best for: path and progress clarity

Brand Story

Orbit Club turns each lesson into a short mission. Kids travel across simple track paths, earn XP fuel, and unlock badges without visual overload.

  • Personality: bold, upbeat, and clear. Navi gives kids a route and cheers each launch.
  • Visual cue: dotted paths, star badges, planets, and mission labels define progress states.
  • Reward language: "Mission complete", "Fuel up with XP", "Next stop: Spanish Colors".
