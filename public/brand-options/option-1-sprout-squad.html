Sprout Squad

A cheerful growth world where every lesson waters a skill. The character system is optimistic, simple, and reward-rich without crowding the screen.

Mascot: Pip Theme: garden progress Best for: cozy daily practice

Brand Story

Sprout Squad frames practice as care. Kids are not grinding lessons, they are helping Pip grow a garden of Math, Vocabulary, and Spanish skills.

  • Personality: encouraging, curious, and gently silly. Pip celebrates effort more than speed.
  • Visual cue: leaves, sprouts, seeds, and watering paths become progress motifs.
  • Reward language: "You grew a new skill", "Water your streak", "One more seed to plant".
