Buddy Blocks

A tactile, build-it-up world where lessons are colorful blocks and every correct answer snaps another piece into place.

Mascot: Moxie Theme: playful building Best for: modular UI systems

Brand Story

Buddy Blocks makes learning feel hands-on. Kids stack progress through small wins, and the same block language can power paths, badges, answers, and profile avatars.

  • Personality: playful, tactile, and confident. Moxie makes progress feel like building something real.
  • Visual cue: blocks, snaps, tabs, tiles, and sticker badges create a strong component vocabulary.
  • Reward language: "Snap in another skill", "Stack your streak", "Build the next answer".
