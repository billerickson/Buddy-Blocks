## Teaching Goal

Guide students to prepare a short oral recitation with a realistic practice plan.

## Student Outcome

The student can choose a target, plan chunked rehearsal, and practice with a clear-voice checklist.

## Misconception Checks

Recitation preparation means repeating the whole piece over and over; performance is only for perfect students; delivery does not need practice.

## Teaching Note

Keep the performance private or low-stakes. The point is prepared recall and clear communication.
