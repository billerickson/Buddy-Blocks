## Teaching Goal

Introduce a short chronological peg sequence for history memory.

## Student Outcome

The student can order the sequence Ancient Egypt, Ancient Greece, Ancient Rome, Middle Ages, Exploration, Early America.

## Misconception Checks

Treating timeline pegs as random facts; putting familiar terms first even if they are later; confusing ancient and early America.

## Teaching Note

Use a horizontal line and place each peg from oldest to latest, emphasizing that the list is a scaffold, not a complete history course.
