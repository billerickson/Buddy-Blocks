## Teaching Goal

Help students identify the type of memory target before recalling the list.

## Student Outcome

The student can decide whether a prompt asks for a saying, measure, grammar definition, compass direction list, geography category, or timeline sequence.

## Misconception Checks

Answering with a familiar list even when the category is wrong; using the same cue for every list; ignoring order when order matters.

## Teaching Note

Show two prompts side by side: "Name an ocean" and "Name a continent." Ask why the same answer cannot fit both.

## Questions

```question

  - measure equivalence

hint: Look for numbers and units.
```

```question
key: u05_l04_q03_fix_wrong_category

acceptedAnswers:
  - The Pacific Ocean is an ocean.
  - Pacific Ocean is an ocean.
explanation: Pacific Ocean belongs in the ocean category, not the continent category.
hint: The word Ocean tells you the category.
```
