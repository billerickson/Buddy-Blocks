## Teaching Goal

Help students sort geography memory targets into continent and ocean categories.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify whether a named place belongs in the continent list or ocean list and recall examples from each.

## Misconception Checks

Mixing continents and oceans; thinking any large place name is the same kind of target; memorizing names without a category cue.

## Teaching Note

Use two headings, "continents" and "oceans," before naming examples. The category is the first cue.
