## Teaching Goal

Show students how delayed review strengthens poem memory and reveals weak chunks.

## Student Outcome

The student can review a learned couplet after a delay, identify a missed chunk, and repair it.

## Misconception Checks

Forgetting after delay means failure; reviewing means rereading the whole poem several times; one missing word ruins the full target.

## Teaching Note

Frame delayed review as a checkup: it shows what is strong and what needs one more focused practice.
