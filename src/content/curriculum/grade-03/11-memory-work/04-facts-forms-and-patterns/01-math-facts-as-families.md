## Teaching Goal

Help students memorize related math facts as connected families rather than isolated facts.

## Student Outcome

The student can recall related multiplication and division facts for small number sets such as 3, 4, and 12.

## Misconception Checks

Memorizing each fact separately; reversing division incorrectly; treating the product as a factor.

## Teaching Note

Put the three numbers 3, 4, and 12 in a triangle and build all related equations from the same set.

## Questions

```question
key: u04_l01_q01_related_facts
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each fact to a related fact from the same family.
pairs:
  - left: 3 x 4 = 12
    right: 12 / 3 = 4
  - left: 4 x 3 = 12
    right: 12 / 4 = 3
  - left: 5 x 6 = 30
    right: 30 / 5 = 6
  - left: 6 x 5 = 30
    right: 30 / 6 = 5
explanation: Multiplication and division facts can use the same three numbers.
hint: Look for the division fact with the same product and factors.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q02_missing_product

prompt: Complete the fact.
sentenceBefore: 3 x 4 =
sentenceAfter: .
choices:
  - "12"
  - "7"
  - "9"
  - "34"
correctAnswer: "12"
explanation: Three groups of four make twelve.
hint: Count 4, 8, 12.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q03_missing_quotient
type: fill-blank
prompt: Complete the related division fact.
sentenceBefore: 12 / 3 =
sentenceAfter: .
choices:
  - "4"
  - "3"
  - "9"
  - "12"
correctAnswer: "4"
explanation: If 3 x 4 = 12, then 12 divided by 3 is 4.
hint: "Ask: 3 times what equals 12?"
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q04_choose_related
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which fact is in the same fact family as 4 x 3 = 12?
choices:
  - 12 / 4 = 3
  - 12 + 4 = 16
  - 4 x 12 = 48
  - 3 - 4 = 1
correctAnswer: 12 / 4 = 3
explanation: The fact family uses 3, 4, and 12 in multiplication and division equations.
hint: Look for a fact using the same three numbers.
```
