## Teaching Goal

Connect food and drink vocabulary to meals and `tener` expressions for hunger and thirst.

## Student Outcome

The student can say or interpret `Tengo hambre`, `Tengo sed`, and simple meal preferences.

## Misconception Checks

Saying `soy hambre`; confusing food with drink responses; treating meal names as identical across cultures.

## Teaching Note

Link `Tengo doce años` and `Tengo hambre` as familiar `tener` chunks without turning the lesson into a full verb chart.
