## Teaching Goal

Teach common foods and drinks with simple polite request chunks.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify foods and drinks and request one politely using `Quiero... por favor` or recognize `Quisiera...` as a polite option.

## Misconception Checks

Treating `quiero` as equally polite in every setting; omitting `por favor`; using wrong articles with food nouns; expecting every food word to be a cognate.

## Teaching Note

Model a cafe exchange and point out how `por favor` changes the tone of a request.
