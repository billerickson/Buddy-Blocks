## Teaching Goal

Teach common weather and season statements as context-setting chunks.

## Student Outcome

The student can interpret and produce common statements such as `Hace sol`, `Hace frío`, `Llueve`, `Nieva`, and `Es invierno`.

## Misconception Checks

Saying `es frío` for weather; treating every weather statement as `hace`; mixing seasons and weather conditions.

## Teaching Note

Present weather phrases with simple icons or contexts and note that students should learn the whole expression.
