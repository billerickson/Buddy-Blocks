## Teaching Goal

Teach limited `ser` chunks for identifying and describing self and others.

## Student Outcome

The student can use `soy`, `eres`, and `es` for names, identity labels, origin, and simple traits in taught contexts.

## Misconception Checks

Using `estoy` for identity; always stating `yo`; translating every English "am/is" the same way.

## Teaching Note

Reuse known introductions, then add `Soy estudiante` and `Ella es Ana` to expand identity language.
