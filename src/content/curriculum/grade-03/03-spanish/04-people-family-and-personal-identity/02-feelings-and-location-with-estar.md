## Teaching Goal

Teach limited `estar` chunks for feelings and location while distinguishing them from `ser` identity.

## Student Outcome

The student can use `estoy` and `está` to say how someone feels or where someone is in familiar contexts.

## Misconception Checks

Using `soy bien`; using `es en la clase`; treating `ser` and `estar` as the same because English uses "is."

## Teaching Note

Contrast `Soy estudiante` with `Estoy bien` and `Estoy en la clase` using clear meanings.
