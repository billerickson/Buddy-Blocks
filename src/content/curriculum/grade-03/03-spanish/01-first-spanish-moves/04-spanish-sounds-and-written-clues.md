## Teaching Goal

Help students notice major Spanish sound-spelling clues in familiar words.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify clear vowel sounds, silent `h`, `ñ`, `j`, `ll/y`, accent marks, and inverted punctuation in familiar examples.

## Misconception Checks

Pronouncing Spanish vowels like English vowels; pronouncing `h` in `hola`; ignoring the opening question mark; treating accent marks as decoration.

## Teaching Note

Use known words such as `hola`, `gracias`, `señor`, `México`, and `¿Cómo?` so the lesson is about noticing, not memorizing new vocabulary.
