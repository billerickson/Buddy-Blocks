## Teaching Goal

Teach students to choose common greetings, farewells, and courtesy phrases for simple social contexts.

## Student Outcome

The student can greet, say goodbye, thank someone, respond to thanks, and make a polite request with familiar chunks.

## Misconception Checks

Using one greeting for every time of day; treating `gracias` and `de nada` as interchangeable; omitting politeness because the phrase still has content.

## Teaching Note

Begin with a two-line exchange such as `Hola. ¿Cómo estás?` and show how greetings and courtesy words make the exchange work.

```question
  - Buenos días
  - Buenas noches
```

