## Teaching Goal

Add plural likes and dislikes to the preference pattern.

## Student Outcome

The student can choose between `me gusta`, `me gustan`, and `no me gusta` for taught preferences.

## Misconception Checks

Using `me gusta` for every plural; placing `no` after the verb; treating `me gustan` as a stronger like.

## Teaching Note

Contrast `Me gusta la clase` with `Me gustan las clases` and `No me gusta la tarea`.
