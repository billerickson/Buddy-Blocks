## Teaching Goal

Teach common classroom nouns with `el` and `la` as part of the noun phrase.

## Student Outcome

The student can recognize classroom objects and choose the correct article for taught nouns.

## Misconception Checks

Treating articles as optional; assuming gender follows object meaning; choosing `el` or `la` by English translation.

## Teaching Note

Introduce each object with its article and repeat it in short classroom scenes.
