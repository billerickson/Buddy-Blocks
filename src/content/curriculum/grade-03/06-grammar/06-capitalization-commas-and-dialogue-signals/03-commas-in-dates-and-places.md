## Teaching Goal

Teach simple comma patterns in dates and place phrases.

## Student Outcome

The student can use commas in dates such as `April 5, 2026` and places such as `Austin, Texas`.

## Misconception Checks

Leaving out the comma between city and state; putting commas after every date word; confusing month/day/year order.

## Teaching Note

Use pattern frames: `Month Day, Year` and `City, State`.

## Questions

```question
key: u06_l03_q01_date_comma
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which date is punctuated correctly?
choices:
  - April 5 2026
  - April, 5 2026
  - April 5, 2026
  - April, 5, 2026
correctAnswer: April 5, 2026
explanation: In the Month Day, Year pattern, place a comma between the day and year.
hint: Use the pattern Month Day, Year.
```

```question
key: u06_l03_q02_place_comma
type: error-correction
prompt: Correct the place punctuation.
sentence: We drove to Tulsa Oklahoma.
acceptedAnswers:
  - We drove to Tulsa, Oklahoma.
explanation: A comma separates the city and state.
hint: Find the city and state.
```

```question
key: u06_l03_q03_fill_date_comma
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the missing punctuation.
sentenceBefore: The race is on May 12
sentenceAfter: "2026."
choices:
  - ","
  - .
  - "?"
  - no punctuation
correctAnswer: ","
explanation: Use a comma between the day and year.
hint: Look for where the day number ends and the year begins.
```

```question
key: u06_l03_q04_match_patterns
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each pattern to an example.
pairs:
  - left: Month Day, Year
    right: June 2, 2026
  - left: City, State
    right: Denver, Colorado
  - left: Month Day, Year in a sentence
    right: The trip is July 4, 2026.
  - left: City, State in a sentence
    right: We visited Austin, Texas.
explanation: Commas group parts of date and place information.
hint: Look for what kind of information is being grouped.
```

```question
key: u06_l03_q05_date_place_cloze
type: multi-blank-cloze
prompt: Complete the date and place punctuation.
parts:
  - We moved to Boise
  - " Idaho. The date was July 4"
  - " 2026."
blanks:
  - correctAnswer: ","
    acceptedAnswers:
      - ","
  - correctAnswer: ","
    acceptedAnswers:
      - ","
explanation: Use one comma between the city and state, and one comma between the day and year.
hint: Find the city/state pair and the month-day-year date.
```

```question
key: u06_l03_q06_explain_place_comma
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Why does Austin, Texas need a comma?
choices:
  - The comma separates the city Austin from the state Texas.
  - The comma shows that Austin owns Texas.
  - The comma separates a subject from a verb.
  - The comma is needed because every two-word place needs one.
correctAnswer: The comma separates the city Austin from the state Texas.
explanation: Commas help group place information for the reader.
hint: Ask what two place parts are next to each other.
```
