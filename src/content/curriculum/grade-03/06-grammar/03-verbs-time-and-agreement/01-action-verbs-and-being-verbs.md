## Teaching Goal

Expand verb recognition beyond visible action to include being verbs.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify action verbs and simple being verbs in predicates.

## Misconception Checks

Missing `is`, `are`, `was`, and `were` as verbs; choosing every exciting word as the verb; confusing nouns that name actions with verbs.

## Teaching Note

Compare `The dog runs` and `The dog is tired`; both predicates need a verb.

## Questions

```question
key: u03_l01_q01_action_verb
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which word is the action verb in this sentence? The rabbit hopped across the yard.
choices:
  - rabbit
  - hopped
  - across
  - yard
correctAnswer: hopped
explanation: Hopped tells what the rabbit did, so it is the action verb.
hint: Ask what the subject did.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q02_being_verb
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which word is the verb in this sentence? The soup is hot.
choices:
  - soup
  - is
  - hot
  - The
correctAnswer: is
explanation: Is is a being verb. It tells what is true about the soup.
hint: A verb can show being, not only action.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q03_action_or_being
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each sentence to the verb job.
pairs:
  - left: The kite soared.
    right: action verb
  - left: The room was quiet.
    right: being verb
  - left: Jamal stirred the soup.
    right: action the subject does
  - left: The apples are ripe.
    right: being that tells what is true
explanation: Action verbs show what the subject does. Being verbs tell what the subject is or is like.
hint: Ask whether the verb shows doing or being.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q04_choose_being_verb
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the verb that completes the sentence.
sentenceBefore: The shells
sentenceAfter: smooth.
choices:
  - are
  - under
  - very
  - shells
correctAnswer: are
explanation: Are is a being verb that connects the subject shells to smooth.
hint: The sentence needs a verb that tells what is true about the shells.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q05_fix_missing_verb
type: error-correction
prompt: Add a verb to make the sentence complete.
sentence: The soup hot.
acceptedAnswers:
  - The soup is hot.
  - The soup was hot.
explanation: The sentence needs a being verb such as is or was.

```
