## Teaching Goal

Show that articles and determiners often signal or clarify nouns.

## Student Outcome

The student can use words such as `a`, `an`, `the`, `this`, `that`, `these`, and `those` to find and choose nouns accurately.

## Misconception Checks

Ignoring articles as meaningless; using `a` before vowel sounds; using `this` with plural nouns or `these` with singular nouns.

## Teaching Note

Read `a apple`, `an apple`, `this books`, and `these books` aloud and ask which sounds and meanings fit.

## Questions

```question
key: u02_l02_q01_choose_article
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the article that fits.
sentenceBefore: Maya ate
sentenceAfter: orange after lunch.
choices:
  - a
  - an
  - these
  - those
correctAnswer: an
explanation: Orange begins with a vowel sound, so an fits before it.
hint: Say the noun aloud and listen to the first sound.
```

```question
key: u02_l02_q02_find_noun_after_signal
type: multiple-choice
prompt: In this sentence, which noun does the point word these signal? These pencils need sharpening.
choices:
  - These
  - pencils
  - need
  - sharpening
correctAnswer: pencils
explanation: These points to the plural noun pencils.
hint: Look right after the determiner for the thing being pointed to.
```

```question
key: u02_l02_q03_match_determiners
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each determiner to a noun phrase that fits.
pairs:
  - left: this
    right: this shell
  - left: these
    right: these shells
  - left: that
    right: that cloud
  - left: those
    right: those clouds
explanation: This and that point to one noun. These and those point to more than one.
hint: Listen for one thing or more than one thing.
```

```question
key: u02_l02_q04_fix_signal_noun
type: error-correction
prompt: Correct the determiner and noun mismatch.
sentence: This books belong on the shelf.
acceptedAnswers:
  - These books belong on the shelf.
  - This book belongs on the shelf.
explanation: This goes with one book. These goes with more than one book.
hint: Decide whether the sentence means one book or more than one.
```

```question
key: u02_l02_q05_article_noun_frame
type: multi-blank-cloze
prompt: Complete the noun-signal sentence.
parts:
  - "The word "
  - " points to one nearby noun, but "
  - " points to more than one nearby noun."
blanks:
  - correctAnswer: this
    choices:
      - this
      - these
      - those
  - correctAnswer: these
    choices:
      - that
      - these
      - a
explanation: This means one nearby thing. These means more than one nearby thing.
hint: Check whether the word points to one nearby thing or more than one nearby thing.
```

```question
key: u02_l02_q06_explain_small_words
type: multiple-choice
prompt: How does the word the help in The puppy slept?
choices:
  - It signals the noun puppy and helps the reader know which puppy.
  - It is the action verb in the sentence.
  - It makes puppy plural.
  - It shows that the puppy owns something.
correctAnswer: It signals the noun puppy and helps the reader know which puppy.
explanation: Articles and determiners may be small, but they help readers find and understand nouns.
hint: Ask what noun comes after the.
```
