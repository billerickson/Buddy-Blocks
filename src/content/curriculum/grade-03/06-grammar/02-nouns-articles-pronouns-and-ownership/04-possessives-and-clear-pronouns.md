## Teaching Goal

Teach singular possessive nouns and clear pronoun reference as relationship tools.

## Student Outcome

The student can show simple ownership with apostrophe-s and choose pronouns that clearly refer to a noun.

## Misconception Checks

Confusing plural `s` with possessive `'s`; putting apostrophes in every word ending with `s`; using `it`, `he`, `she`, or `they` when several nouns could fit.

## Teaching Note

Draw an ownership arrow from `Maya's pencil` to Maya, then draw a pronoun arrow from `she` back to a named noun.

## Questions

```question
key: u02_l04_q01_possessive_meaning
type: multiple-choice
prompt: What does the phrase Liam's backpack mean?
choices:
  - one backpack that belongs to Liam
  - more than one backpack
  - a backpack near Liam
  - Liam carrying a backpack
correctAnswer: one backpack that belongs to Liam
explanation: Apostrophe-s after Liam shows ownership.
hint: Ask whose backpack it is.
```

```question
key: u02_l04_q02_fix_possessive
type: error-correction
prompt: Correct the sentence to show ownership.
sentence: The girls notebook fell under the desk.
acceptedAnswers:
  - The girl's notebook fell under the desk.
explanation: Girl's shows that the notebook belongs to one girl.
hint: Add apostrophe-s to the owner.
```

```question
key: u02_l04_q03_pronoun_reference
type: passage-question
prompt: Read the sentences and answer.
passageTitle: A Clear Pronoun
passage: |
  Sofia picked up the shell.
  She put it in her pocket.
question: What does She refer to?
choices:
  - Sofia
  - shell
  - pocket
  - picked
correctAnswer: Sofia
explanation: She refers to Sofia, the person named in the first sentence.
hint: Find the noun that the pronoun could replace.
```

```question
key: u02_l04_q04_unclear_pronoun
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which revision makes the pronoun clear? Ana handed Maya the book because she needed it.
choices:
  - Ana handed Maya the book because Maya needed it.
  - Ana handed Maya it because she needed the book.
  - She handed Maya the book because she needed it.
  - Ana handed she the book because Maya needed it.
correctAnswer: Ana handed Maya the book because Maya needed it.
explanation: Naming Maya tells the reader who needed the book.
hint: Replace the unclear pronoun with the noun it means.
```

```question
key: u02_l04_q05_possessive_or_plural
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each phrase to its meaning.
pairs:
  - left: the dog's bowl
    right: one dog owns the bowl
  - left: the dogs bark
    right: more than one dog barks
  - left: Maya's pencil
    right: Maya owns the pencil
  - left: three pencils
    right: more than one pencil
explanation: Apostrophes show ownership in these examples. Plural nouns show more than one.
hint: Ask whether the phrase shows whose or how many.
```

```question
key: u02_l04_q06_explain_pronoun
type: multiple-choice
prompt: "Which revision makes the sentence clearly refer to Eli: Noah showed Eli his drawing?"
choices:
  - Noah showed Eli the drawing Eli made.
  - Noah showed Eli his drawing.
  - Noah showed Eli Noah's drawing.
  - He showed Eli his drawing.
correctAnswer: Noah showed Eli the drawing Eli made.
explanation: When a pronoun could mean more than one person, naming the person makes the sentence clearer.
hint: Name Eli as the person connected to the drawing.
```
