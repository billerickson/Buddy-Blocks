## Teaching Goal

Teach adjectives as words that modify nouns by telling which one, what kind, or how many.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify adjectives and the nouns they describe, then choose a precise adjective for meaning.

## Misconception Checks

Treating any nice-sounding word as an adjective; choosing the noun instead of its modifier; adding irrelevant adjectives.

## Teaching Note

Use `The dog barked` and ask which word could tell what kind of dog without changing the sentence core.

## Questions

  - hung

  - door
