## Teaching Goal

Use doubles to derive related multiplication facts.

## Student Outcome

The student can use known doubles, such as 4 x 2 or 6 x 2, to reason about nearby facts.

## Key Ideas

Doubles and near doubles can be represented by arrays split into equal or nearly equal parts.

## Misconceptions

Doubling the wrong factor; treating near doubles as exact doubles; losing one group when adjusting.
