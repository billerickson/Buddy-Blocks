## Teaching Goal

Use distributive reasoning to solve harder multiplication facts.

## Student Outcome

The student can decompose one factor, find partial products, and combine them to get the total product.

## Key Ideas

A larger array can be split into two smaller arrays whose products add to the original product.

## Misconceptions

Splitting both factors without tracking parts; adding factors instead of products; losing rows or columns in the split.
