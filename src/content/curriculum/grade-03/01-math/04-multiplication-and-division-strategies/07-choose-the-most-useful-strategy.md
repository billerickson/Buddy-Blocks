## Teaching Goal

Develop judgment about which multiplication or division strategy fits a fact.

## Student Outcome

The student can choose and justify a useful strategy for a given fact.

## Key Ideas

Strategy choice depends on the numbers and structure, not on a fixed rule.

## Misconceptions

Using the same strategy for every fact; choosing the fastest-looking shortcut without understanding; ignoring division relationships.
