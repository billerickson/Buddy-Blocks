## Teaching Goal

Practice mixed multiplication and division facts while keeping meaning and relationships visible.

## Student Outcome

The student can solve mixed facts accurately and identify a supporting related fact or strategy when needed.

## Key Ideas

Fluency includes accurate recall, flexible strategy use, and retention over time.

## Misconceptions

Speed matters more than accuracy; one correct answer proves mastery; division facts do not need labels or related facts.
