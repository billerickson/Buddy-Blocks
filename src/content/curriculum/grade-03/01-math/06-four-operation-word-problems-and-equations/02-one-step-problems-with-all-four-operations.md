## Teaching Goal

Practice choosing and solving one-step problems across addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division.

## Student Outcome

The student can match a one-step context to the correct operation, model, equation, and answer.

## Key Ideas

Different operations answer different relationship questions.

## Misconceptions

Applying the most recently learned operation to every problem; selecting equations by numbers only; omitting units.

## Questions
