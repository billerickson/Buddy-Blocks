## Teaching Goal

Represent multiplication language as "number of groups" and "size of each group."

## Student Outcome

The student can model and describe statements such as 5 groups of 3 using drawings and repeated addition.

## Key Ideas

"5 groups of 3" means five equal groups, each with three items.

## Misconceptions

Reversing groups and group size in the story; drawing unequal groups; counting the number of groups as the total.

## Questions

  - "6 + 4"

correctAnswer: "6 groups with 4 in each group"

hint: "The first number tells how many groups."
questionGoal: "Interpret \"groups of\" language."
misconception: "Adding the two numbers or reversing without context."
