## Teaching Goal

Help students identify when multiplication is appropriate by checking for equal-size groups.

## Student Outcome

The student can decide whether a situation shows equal groups and explain why multiplication does or does not fit.

## Key Ideas

Multiplication requires groups of the same size.

## Misconceptions

Multiplying any groups even when the sizes differ; focusing on total objects only; assuming repeated contexts are always multiplication.
