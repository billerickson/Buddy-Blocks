## Teaching Goal

Demonstrate retained arithmetic skill and reasonableness checking across all four operations.

## Student Outcome

The student can solve mixed operation problems and judge whether answers fit the situation.

## Key Ideas

Operation meaning, estimation, units, and inverse relationships work together in mixed problem solving.

## Misconceptions

Choosing operations by key words; trusting every computed answer; forgetting units or intermediate quantities.
