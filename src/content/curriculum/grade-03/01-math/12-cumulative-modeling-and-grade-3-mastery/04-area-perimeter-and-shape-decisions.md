## Teaching Goal

Review geometry and geometric measurement decision-making.

## Student Outcome

The student can decide whether a problem involves area, perimeter, shape attributes, or equal partitions and solve accordingly.

## Key Ideas

Geometry tasks require identifying the attribute or property being asked about.

## Misconceptions

Mixing area and perimeter; classifying by appearance; using length units for area or square units for perimeter.
