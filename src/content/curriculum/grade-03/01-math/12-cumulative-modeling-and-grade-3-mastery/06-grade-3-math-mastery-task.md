## Teaching Goal

Gather compact evidence of end-of-level mastery across major Grade 3 domains.

## Student Outcome

The student can solve a mixed-domain task requiring representation choice, computation, explanation, and checking.

## Key Ideas

Grade 3 mastery combines fluency, conceptual understanding, application, and transfer.

## Misconceptions

Treating the task as only computation; skipping model choice; forgetting to check reasonableness or units.

## Questions

```question
key: u12_l07_q01_capstone_operations
type: passage-question
prompt: "Read the story and answer."
passageTitle: "Field Trip Packs"
passage: "There are 8 bags with 6 snacks in each bag. Students eat 14 snacks."
question: "How many snacks are left?"
choices: ["34", "48", "62", "86"]
correctAnswer: "34"
explanation: "8 x 6 = 48 snacks, and 48 - 14 = 34."
hint: "Find the total snacks first."
questionGoal: "Assess mixed two-step operation mastery."
misconception: "Adding all numbers or stopping after multiplication."
```
