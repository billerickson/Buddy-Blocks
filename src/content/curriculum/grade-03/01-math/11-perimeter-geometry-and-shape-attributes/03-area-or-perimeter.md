## Teaching Goal

Distinguish area as square-unit covering from perimeter as boundary length.

## Student Outcome

The student can decide whether a task asks for area or perimeter and choose the correct method.

## Key Ideas

Area measures inside with square units; perimeter measures around with length units.

## Misconception Checks

Using multiplication for every rectangle question; adding sides for area; counting squares for perimeter.

## Teaching Note

Use the same rectangle for two questions: "cover it" and "fence around it."
