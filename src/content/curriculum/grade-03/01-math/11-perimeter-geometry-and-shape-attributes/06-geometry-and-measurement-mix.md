## Teaching Goal

Integrate perimeter, area, shape attributes, and fraction partitioning in mixed tasks.

## Student Outcome

The student can choose the correct geometry or measurement idea for a prompt and explain the choice.

## Key Ideas

Shape tasks may ask about inside covering, outside boundary, attributes, or equal parts.

## Misconceptions

Applying area or perimeter automatically to every shape question; ignoring the question word; classifying by appearance.
