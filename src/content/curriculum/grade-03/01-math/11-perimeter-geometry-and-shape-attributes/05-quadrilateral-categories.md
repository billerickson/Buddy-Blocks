## Teaching Goal

Reason about quadrilaterals and subcategories using attributes.

## Student Outcome

The student can classify rectangles, squares, rhombuses, and other quadrilaterals by Grade 3 attributes.

## Key Ideas

Shapes can belong to broad categories and more specific subcategories.

## Misconception Checks

A square is not a rectangle; a shape changes category when tilted; every quadrilateral is a square or rectangle.

## Teaching Note

Use an attribute chart with columns for four sides, opposite sides, equal sides, and square corners when appropriate.
