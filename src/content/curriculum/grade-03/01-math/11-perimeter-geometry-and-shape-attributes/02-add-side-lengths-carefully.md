## Teaching Goal

Find perimeter by adding all side lengths, including controlled missing repeated sides.

## Student Outcome

The student can calculate perimeter for rectangles and simple polygons without skipping sides.

## Key Ideas

Perimeter is the sum of every side length around the boundary.

## Misconception Checks

Adding only two sides; multiplying when addition is clearer; assuming opposite sides without evidence in non-rectangles.

## Teaching Note

Put a check mark on each side as it is included in the perimeter sum.
