## Teaching Goal

Define perimeter as the distance around a shape's boundary.

## Student Outcome

The student can trace a boundary and find perimeter when side lengths are given or countable.

## Key Ideas

Perimeter measures around the outside, not the inside covering.

## Misconception Checks

Counting inside squares; confusing perimeter with area; omitting one side or counting a side twice.

## Teaching Note

Use a finger-trace around a rectangle and say each side length aloud before adding.
