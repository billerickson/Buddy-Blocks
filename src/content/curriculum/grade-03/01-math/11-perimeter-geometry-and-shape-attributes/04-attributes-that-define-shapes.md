## Teaching Goal

Classify shapes by defining attributes rather than appearance.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify attributes such as number of sides, straight sides, and equal sides and use them to classify shapes.

## Key Ideas

Defining attributes determine shape categories; color, size, and orientation do not.

## Misconception Checks

A rotated shape is a different shape; color or size changes category; all four-sided shapes are rectangles.

## Teaching Note

Show several triangles in different sizes and orientations and ask what they all share.

## Questions

```question
key: u11_l04_q01_defining_attribute
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which is a defining attribute of a triangle?
choices:
  - 3 sides
  - blue color
  - large size
  - tilted position
correctAnswer: 3 sides
explanation: A triangle is defined by having 3 sides.
hint: Defining attributes do not change with color or size.
```
