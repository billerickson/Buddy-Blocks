## Teaching Goal

Classify shapes by defining attributes rather than appearance.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify attributes such as number of sides, straight sides, and equal sides and use them to classify shapes.

## Key Ideas

Defining attributes determine shape categories; color, size, and orientation do not.

## Misconceptions

A rotated shape is a different shape; color or size changes category; all four-sided shapes are rectangles.
