## Teaching Goal

Extend rounding to the nearest hundred and connect rounding to reasonableness.

## Student Outcome

The student can round to the nearest hundred and use the rounded number to judge approximate size.

## Key Ideas

Hundreds benchmarks help estimate and check whether an answer is in the right range.

## Misconceptions

Rounding 349 to 400 because 9 is large; ignoring the midpoint between hundreds; treating rounding as exact computation.
