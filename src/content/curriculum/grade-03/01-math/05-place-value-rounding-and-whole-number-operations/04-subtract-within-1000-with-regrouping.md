## Teaching Goal

Connect multi-digit subtraction to decomposing place-value units.

## Student Outcome

The student can subtract within 1,000 using place-value reasoning and explain necessary regrouping.

## Key Ideas

Regrouping in subtraction means trading one larger unit for ten smaller units.

## Misconception Checks

Subtracting smaller digits from larger digits regardless of position; regrouping without changing the value; losing track of zeros.

## Teaching Note

Use base-ten drawings to show why 302 - 158 requires decomposing a hundred into tens and ones.
