## Teaching Goal

Connect basic multiplication facts to multiplying one-digit numbers by multiples of 10.

## Student Outcome

The student can solve facts such as 6 x 40 by using 6 x 4 tens.

## Key Ideas

6 x 40 means 6 groups of 4 tens, or 24 tens, which is 240.

## Misconceptions

Adding a zero without understanding; multiplying only the visible nonzero digits and dropping units; treating 6 x 40 as unrelated to 6 x 4.
