## Teaching Goal

Use 0, 1/2, and 1 as benchmarks for fraction comparison.

## Student Outcome

The student can compare Grade 3 fractions by locating them near familiar benchmarks when helpful.

## Key Ideas

Benchmarks help judge fraction size without exact calculation.

## Misconceptions

Benchmark labels are arbitrary; every comparison must use numerator/denominator rules; 1/2 is always one tick mark.

## Questions
