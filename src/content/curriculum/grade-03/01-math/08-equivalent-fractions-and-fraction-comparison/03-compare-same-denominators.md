## Teaching Goal

Compare fractions with the same denominator by counting same-size parts.

## Student Outcome

The student can decide which same-denominator fraction is greater and explain why.

## Key Ideas

If the denominator is the same, the parts are the same size, so the larger numerator means more parts.

## Misconception Checks

Comparing denominators even when equal; thinking the smaller numerator is closer to 1; ignoring same whole.

## Teaching Note

Use two equal fraction strips divided into the same number of parts and shade different numerators.
