## Teaching Goal

Help students select the appropriate comparison reasoning for a fraction pair.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify whether to use same denominator, same numerator, benchmark, equivalence, or same-whole reasoning.

## Key Ideas

Different fraction pairs call for different valid reasoning.

## Misconceptions

One rule works for every comparison; cross-multiplication is needed; equivalent fractions are comparisons of different amounts.

## Questions
