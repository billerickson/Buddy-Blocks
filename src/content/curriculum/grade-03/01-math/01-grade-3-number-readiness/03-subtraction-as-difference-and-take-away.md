## Teaching Goal

Review subtraction as both taking away and finding the difference.

## Student Outcome

The student can solve subtraction problems using count-back, count-up, place-value, or number-line reasoning.

## Key Ideas

A number line can show subtraction as moving back or as the distance between two numbers.

## Misconceptions

Believing subtraction always means "take away"; subtracting smaller digits from larger digits regardless of place; forgetting subtraction can check addition.

misconception: "Thinking subtraction only means taking away."
