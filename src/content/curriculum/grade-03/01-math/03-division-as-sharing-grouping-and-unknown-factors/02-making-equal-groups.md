## Teaching Goal

Introduce division as making equal groups of a known size.

## Student Outcome

The student can model a grouping division problem and identify how many groups can be made.

## Key Ideas

When the size of each group is known, division can find the number of groups.

## Misconceptions

Confusing group size with number of groups; stopping before all objects are grouped; treating grouping and sharing as identical.
