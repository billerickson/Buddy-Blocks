## Teaching Goal

Help students decide which division interpretation fits a problem situation.

## Student Outcome

The student can classify division contexts as sharing or grouping and solve with an appropriate model.

## Key Ideas

Division can answer "how many in each group?" or "how many groups?"

## Misconception Checks

Treating all division as sharing; selecting an equation without interpreting the question; omitting labels from the quotient.

## Teaching Note

Ask students to underline the question words that reveal whether group count or group size is unknown.

## Questions

```question
key: u03_l07_q01_choose_sharing

prompt: Which problem asks "how many in each group?"

  - 24 stickers shared equally among 6 friends
  - 24 stickers packed 6 per page
  - 6 stickers plus 24 stickers

explanation: The number of friends is known, so the unknown is how many each friend gets.
hint: Look for sharing among a known number of groups.
```
