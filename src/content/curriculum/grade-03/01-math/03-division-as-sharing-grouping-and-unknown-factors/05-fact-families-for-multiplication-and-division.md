## Teaching Goal

Build multiplication/division fact families from one equal-group relationship.

## Student Outcome

The student can generate or complete related multiplication and division equations using the same three numbers.

## Key Ideas

A fact family shows how two factors and a product are connected by multiplication and division.

## Misconceptions

Including unrelated numbers; writing invalid division turnarounds; assuming every number order makes a true equation.
