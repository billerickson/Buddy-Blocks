## Teaching Goal

Use multiplication or unknown-factor reasoning in rectangular area contexts.

## Student Outcome

The student can find a missing area or missing side length in supported rectangle problems.

## Key Ideas

Area, rows, and columns form a multiplication relationship; a missing side can be found as an unknown factor.

## Misconceptions

Dividing the wrong numbers; treating missing side as perimeter; guessing a side without checking the product.

## Questions

key: u10_l05_q01_missing_side
type: multiple-choice
prompt: "A rectangle has area 36 square units and one side length 6. What is the other side length?"
