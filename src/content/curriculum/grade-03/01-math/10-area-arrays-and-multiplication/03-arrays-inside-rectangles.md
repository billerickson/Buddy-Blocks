## Teaching Goal

Connect rectangular area to multiplication arrays.

## Student Outcome

The student can write a multiplication equation for the area of a tiled rectangle.

## Key Ideas

A tiled rectangle is an array of square units; rows times columns gives the total square units.

## Misconception Checks

Multiplying side labels without understanding the square units; adding rows and columns; forgetting square-unit labels.

## Teaching Note

Place an array from Unit 2 inside a rectangle and relabel the dots as square units.
