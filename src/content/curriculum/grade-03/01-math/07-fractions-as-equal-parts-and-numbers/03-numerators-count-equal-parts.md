## Teaching Goal

Build non-unit fractions as counts of unit fractions.

## Student Outcome

The student can name and represent fractions such as 3/4 as three one-fourth parts.

## Key Ideas

The numerator counts how many equal parts are being named.

## Misconceptions

Numerator counts all parts in the whole; denominator changes when more parts are shaded; 3/4 means 3 wholes and 4 parts.
