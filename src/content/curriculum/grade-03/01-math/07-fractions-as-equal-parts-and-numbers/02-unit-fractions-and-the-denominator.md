## Teaching Goal

Introduce unit fractions and denominator meaning.

## Student Outcome

The student can name one equal part as a unit fraction and explain what the denominator tells.

## Key Ideas

In 1/4, the denominator 4 means the whole is partitioned into 4 equal parts.

## Misconceptions

Denominator counts shaded parts; larger denominator means larger part; denominator and numerator are unrelated labels.
