## Teaching Goal

Extend fraction number lines beyond 1 in controlled cases.

## Student Outcome

The student can locate and name fractions greater than 1 when intervals continue past one whole.

## Key Ideas

Equal fractional intervals continue past 1 just as whole-number intervals continue past each whole.

## Misconceptions

Fractions cannot be greater than 1; the count restarts after 1; 5/4 must be less than 1 because it is a fraction.
