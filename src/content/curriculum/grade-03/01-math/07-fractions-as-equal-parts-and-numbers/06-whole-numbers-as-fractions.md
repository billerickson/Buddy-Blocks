## Teaching Goal

Show that whole numbers can be named with fraction notation.

## Student Outcome

The student can recognize 1 as 2/2, 3/3, 4/4, and similar whole fractions.

## Key Ideas

When all equal parts of one whole are counted, the fraction names the whole.

## Misconception Checks

A fraction can never equal a whole number; 4/4 is four wholes; denominator always makes the amount less than 1.

## Teaching Note

Shade every fourth in a whole and label the total as four fourths and one whole.
