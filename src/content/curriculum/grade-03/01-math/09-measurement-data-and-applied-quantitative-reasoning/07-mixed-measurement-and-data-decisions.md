## Teaching Goal

Integrate units, graphs, time, measurement, data, and arithmetic in applied contexts.

## Student Outcome

The student can choose the needed representation or operation for mixed measurement and data problems.

## Key Ideas

Applied math requires reading the context, units, scale, and question before computing.

## Misconceptions

Using numbers without units; ignoring graph scales; applying the most recent measurement method to every problem.

## Questions

```question
key: u09_l09_q01_choose_display_operation
type: multiple-choice
prompt: "A graph key says each picture = 4 students. A row has 6 pictures. What should you do?"

explanation: "Each picture counts for 4 students, so use the scale."
hint: "The key tells the value of one picture."
questionGoal: "Choose operation from scaled graph context."
misconception: "Counting pictures as one or adding picture count and scale."
```
