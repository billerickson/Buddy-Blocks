## Teaching Goal

Connect length measurement, estimation, and appropriate units.

## Student Outcome

The student can choose a length unit, read or reason about a measurement, and judge whether it is reasonable.

## Key Ideas

Length is measured by equal units laid end to end with no gaps or overlaps.

## Misconception Checks

Starting at 1 on a ruler instead of 0; mixing units; choosing unreasonable units for the object.

## Teaching Note

Compare measuring a pencil in centimeters with measuring a room in meters.
