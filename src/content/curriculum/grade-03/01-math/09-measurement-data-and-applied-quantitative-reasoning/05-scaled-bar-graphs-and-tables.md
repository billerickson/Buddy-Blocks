## Teaching Goal

Interpret scaled bar graphs and tables using intervals.

## Student Outcome

The student can read values, compare categories, and answer total/difference questions from scaled displays.

## Key Ideas

Bars and table values must be read according to the scale or interval.

## Misconceptions

Reading unlabeled grid lines as ones; using bar height only without the scale; comparing visually without calculating.
