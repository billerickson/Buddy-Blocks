## Teaching Goal

Solve simple mass and liquid-volume problems with appropriate units.

## Student Outcome

The student can interpret grams, kilograms, liters, and milliliters in short problem contexts.

## Key Ideas

Mass and liquid volume measure different attributes and require different units.

## Misconceptions

Confusing mass with volume; comparing quantities with different units as if labels do not matter; choosing kilogram or liter by object size alone.
