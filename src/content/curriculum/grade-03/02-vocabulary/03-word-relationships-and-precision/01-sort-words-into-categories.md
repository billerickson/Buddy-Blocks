## Teaching Goal

Strengthen category reasoning so students group words by meaning rather than by surface features.

## Student Outcome

The student can sort words into categories and identify a word that does not belong.

## Misconception Checks

Sorting by first letter or sound; treating a category label as a member; ignoring a word's meaning.

## Teaching Note

Sort words such as robin, eagle, salmon, and sparrow and discuss why one does not belong.

## Questions

```question
key: u03_l01_q01_category_match
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each word to its category.
pairs:
  - left: sparrow
    right: bird
  - left: hammer
    right: tool
  - left: triangle
    right: shape
  - left: carrot
    right: vegetable
explanation: A category names a group, and each word is a member of that group.
hint: Ask what kind of thing each word is.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q02_odd_one_out
type: multiple-choice
prompt: "Which word does not belong with the others: river, lake, ocean, ladder?"
choices:
  - ladder
  - river
  - lake
  - ocean
correctAnswer: ladder
explanation: River, lake, and ocean are bodies of water. Ladder is not.
hint: Find the three words that share a category.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q03_category_label
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the category label.
sentenceBefore: Robins, eagles, and owls are all
sentenceAfter: .
choices:
  - birds
  - insects
  - tools
  - rocks
correctAnswer: birds
explanation: Robins, eagles, and owls are all kinds of birds.
hint: Think about what all three animals have in common.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q04_category_not_label
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence uses a category label correctly?
choices:
  - '"Mammal" is a category that includes dogs and whales.'
  - '"Dog" is the category for all mammals.'
  - '"Whale" means every animal.'
  - '"Mammal" is only one kind of dog.'
correctAnswer: '"Mammal" is a category that includes dogs and whales.'
explanation: Mammal is the group name; dogs and whales are members of the group.
hint: A category label names the whole group.
```

