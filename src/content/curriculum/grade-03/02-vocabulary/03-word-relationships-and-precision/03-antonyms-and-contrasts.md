## Teaching Goal

Teach antonyms as meaning opposites or strong contrasts, especially in sentence context.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify an antonym or contrast word and use it to understand a sentence.

## Misconception Checks

Choosing any different word as an antonym; missing degree words such as tiny versus enormous; treating contrast as always exact opposite.

## Teaching Note

Use a sentence pair such as "The pond was shallow near the edge but deep in the middle."

## Questions


```question

  - full
  - square
  - brown
  - nearby

hint: Look for the opposite of empty.
```
