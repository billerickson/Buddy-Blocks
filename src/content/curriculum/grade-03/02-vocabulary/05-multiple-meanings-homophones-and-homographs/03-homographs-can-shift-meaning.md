## Teaching Goal

Teach students that some same-spelled words can have different meanings, and sometimes different pronunciations.

## Student Outcome

The student can use context to interpret a common homograph.

## Misconception Checks

Same spelling means same meaning; pronunciation cannot change; sentence meaning is not needed if the word is familiar.

## Teaching Note

Use accessible examples such as `wind` as moving air and `wind` a string, or `bow` on a gift and `bow` after a performance.

## Questions

```question
key: u05_l03_q01_wind_meaning
type: multiple-choice
prompt: 'Read: "The wind blew leaves across the yard." Which meaning of wind fits?'
choices:
  - moving air
  - to twist or turn
  - a string on a toy
  - a path through woods
correctAnswer: moving air
explanation: Blew leaves is a clue that wind means moving air.
hint: Ask what can blow leaves.
```

```question
key: u05_l03_q02_homograph_match
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each sentence to the meaning of bow.
pairs:
  - left: She tied a bow on the gift.
    right: a looped knot
  - left: The actor took a bow.
    right: a bend at the waist
  - left: He used a bow to shoot an arrow.
    right: a curved weapon
  - left: The bow of the boat cut through the water.
    right: the front of a boat
explanation: The same spelling can have several meanings.
hint: Use the object or action in each sentence.
```

```question
key: u05_l03_q03_close_meaning
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the meaning that fits.
sentenceBefore: In "The store will close at six," close means
sentenceAfter: .
choices:
  - shut
  - near
  - careful
  - friendly
correctAnswer: shut
explanation: A store can close, or shut, at a certain time.
hint: Ask what happens to a store at the end of the day.
```

```question
key: u05_l03_q04_pronunciation_without_symbols
type: multiple-choice
prompt: 'Read: "Please wind the string around the spool." Which clue shows wind means twist or wrap?'
choices:
  - around the spool
  - Please
  - the string
  - Read
correctAnswer: around the spool
explanation: Around the spool shows wind means wrap or twist, not moving air.
hint: Look for the words that show an action.
```
