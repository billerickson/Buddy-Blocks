## Teaching Goal

Teach students to recognize direct definitions and restatements as context clues.

## Student Outcome

The student can infer a word meaning when the sentence explains the word in nearby language.

## Misconception Checks

Ignoring the explanation after a comma or dash; choosing a nearby but unrelated word; thinking restatement clues must use exactly the same words.

## Teaching Note

Show a sentence such as "A habitat, or place where an animal lives, gives an animal food and shelter" and underline the explaining words.

## Questions

```question
key: u01_l02_q01_definition_clue
type: multiple-choice
prompt: 'Read: "A nocturnal animal is active at night." What does nocturnal mean?'
choices:
  - Active at night
  - Very small
  - Living in water
  - Able to fly
correctAnswer: Active at night
explanation: The words is active at night directly define nocturnal.
hint: Look after the word is.
```

```question
key: u01_l02_q02_restatement_signal
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the word that signals a restatement clue.
sentenceBefore: A calf,
sentenceAfter: a young cow, stayed close to its mother.
choices:
  - or
  - because
  - but
  - before
correctAnswer: or
explanation: The word or can signal that the next words restate or explain the unknown word.
hint: Which word can mean "in other words"?
```

```question
key: u01_l02_q03_match_restatement
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each word to the restatement clue that explains it.
pairs:
  - left: boulder
    right: a large rock
  - left: purchase
    right: buy
  - left: assist
    right: help
  - left: ancient
    right: very old
explanation: A restatement clue says the same idea in easier words.
hint: Match by meaning, not by first letter.
```

```question
key: u01_l02_q04_sentence_definition
type: passage-question
prompt: Read the sentence and answer.
passageTitle: Science Class
passage: The students observed condensation, drops of water that formed on the outside of the cold glass.
question: What does condensation mean in this sentence?
choices:
  - Drops of water that formed on the glass
  - A loud sound from a glass
  - A warm breeze
  - A kind of pencil mark
correctAnswer: Drops of water that formed on the glass
explanation: The phrase after the comma explains condensation.
hint: Look at the words right after the comma.
```

```question
key: u01_l02_q05_find_clue_words
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which part of the sentence explains the word sturdy? "The sturdy bridge, strong enough to hold heavy trucks, crossed the river."
choices:
  - strong enough to hold heavy trucks
  - crossed the river
  - The sturdy bridge
  - heavy trucks
correctAnswer: strong enough to hold heavy trucks
explanation: "That phrase restates what sturdy means: strong and solid."
hint: Find the part that tells what kind of bridge it is.
```

```question
key: u01_l02_q06_complete_restatement
type: multi-blank-cloze
prompt: Complete the sentence with a word and its restatement.
parts:
  - "The room was "
  - ", or "
  - .
blanks:
  - correctAnswer: tidy
    choices:
      - tidy
      - stormy
      - hungry
  - correctAnswer: neat and clean
    choices:
      - neat and clean
      - loud and wild
      - hard to lift
explanation: Tidy means neat and clean, and or signals a restatement.
hint: The second blank should explain the first blank.
```
