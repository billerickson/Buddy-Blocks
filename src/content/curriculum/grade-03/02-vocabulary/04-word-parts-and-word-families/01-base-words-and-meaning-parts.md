## Teaching Goal

Teach students to identify a base word and use it as the meaning center of a longer word.

## Student Outcome

The student can find the base word in familiar words and explain the related meaning.

## Misconception Checks

Treating any small spelling chunk as a base word; ignoring spelling changes; assuming look-alike words are related.

## Teaching Note

Show `teach`, `teacher`, and `teaching` and ask what meaning stays the same.

## Questions

```question
key: u04_l01_q01_base_word
type: multiple-choice
prompt: What is the base word in helpful?
choices:
  - help
  - ful
  - he
  - helpful
correctAnswer: help
explanation: Help is the base word. The suffix -ful is added to it.
hint: Find the word that can stand alone.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q02_derived_match
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each longer word to its base word.
pairs:
  - left: teacher
    right: teach
  - left: playful
    right: play
  - left: kindness
    right: kind
  - left: careless
    right: care
explanation: The base word carries the main meaning inside the longer word.
hint: Look for the word that can stand by itself.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q03_base_meaning
type: fill-blank
prompt: Use the base word to complete the meaning.
sentenceBefore: A painter is a person who
sentenceAfter: .
choices:
  - paints
  - runs
  - teaches
  - measures
correctAnswer: paints
explanation: Painter contains the base word paint, so a painter is a person who paints.
hint: Find the base word inside painter.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q04_context_base
type: passage-question
prompt: Read the passage and use the base word.
passageTitle: Garden Helper
passage: Maya was a gardener. She planted seeds, pulled weeds, and watered the flowers.
question: How does the base word garden help you understand gardener?
choices:
  - A gardener is a person who works in a garden
  - A gardener is a kind of flower
  - A gardener means water
  - A gardener is a place to sit
correctAnswer: A gardener is a person who works in a garden
explanation: The base word garden and the sentence clues show what a gardener does.
hint: Look at the work Maya does.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q05_not_a_base
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which explanation is best?
choices:
  - '"Corner" is not usually corn + er because a corner is not a person who corns.'
  - '"Corner" always means a person who grows corn.'
  - Every word ending in er names a person.
  - The base word in corner is always cone.
correctAnswer: '"Corner" is not usually corn + er because a corner is not a person who corns.'
explanation: Not every ending that looks like a suffix is really a meaningful suffix.
hint: Check whether the parts make a sensible meaning.
```

key: u04_l01_q06_base_explanation

  - correctAnswer: read

      - read
