## Teaching Goal

Teach common suffixes as endings that can change meaning or how a word is used in a sentence.

## Student Outcome

The student can use suffixes such as `-ful`, `-less`, `-ly`, `-er`, `-est`, and `-ness` to infer meaning or word use.

## Misconception Checks

Thinking all suffixes do the same job; treating `-er` only as a person ending; ignoring how the word works in the sentence.

## Teaching Note

Compare `careful`, `careless`, and `carefully` in short sentences.

## Questions

```question
key: u04_l03_q01_suffix_match
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each suffix to a common meaning or use.
pairs:
  - left: -ful
    right: full of
  - left: -less
    right: without
  - left: -ly
    right: in a certain way
  - left: -ness
    right: state or quality
explanation: Suffixes are meaningful endings added to base words.
hint: Think of joyful, fearless, slowly, and kindness.
```

```question
key: u04_l03_q02_suffix_meaning
type: multiple-choice
prompt: What does fearless mean?
choices:
  - without fear
  - full of fear
  - fear again
  - before fear
correctAnswer: without fear
explanation: The suffix -less means without, so fearless means without fear.
hint: Use the meaning of -less.
```

```question
key: u04_l03_q03_suffix_sentence
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the word that fits the sentence.
sentenceBefore: The team celebrated
sentenceAfter: after winning the final game.
choices:
  - joyfully
  - joyless
  - joyness
  - prejoy
correctAnswer: joyfully
explanation: Joyfully means in a joyful way, which fits celebrated.
hint: Choose the word that tells how they celebrated.
```

```question
key: u04_l03_q04_er_est
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence uses -est to compare three or more things?
choices:
  - That is the tallest tower in the city.
  - This tower is taller than mine.
  - The tower has windows.
  - The tower was built yesterday.
correctAnswer: That is the tallest tower in the city.
explanation: Tallest uses -est to show the greatest height among a group.
hint: Look for the word ending in -est.
```

```question
key: u04_l03_q05_suffix_context
type: passage-question
prompt: Read the passage and use the suffix.
passageTitle: Careful Work
passage: Lena carried the full glass carefully so she would not spill a drop.
question: What does carefully tell you?
choices:
  - how Lena carried the glass
  - where Lena found the glass
  - what color the glass was
  - when Lena drank the water
correctAnswer: how Lena carried the glass
explanation: The suffix -ly often helps a word tell how an action is done.
hint: Ask what carefully describes in the sentence.
```

      - without hope
