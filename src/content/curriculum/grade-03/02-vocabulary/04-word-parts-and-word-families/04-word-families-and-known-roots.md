## Teaching Goal

Teach students to connect related words by shared base or known root meaning.

## Student Outcome

The student can group word-family members and infer a related word's meaning from the family.

## Misconception Checks

Grouping words by spelling only; assuming every look-alike word is related; missing meaning changes when affixes are added.

## Teaching Note

Build a family web around `act`: act, action, active, actor, and react, using context for each.

## Questions

```question
key: u04_l04_q01_word_family
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which word belongs in the act word family?
choices:
  - action
  - apple
  - corner
  - river
correctAnswer: action
explanation: Action shares the base act and is related in meaning.
hint: Look for a word that shares both letters and meaning with act.
```
