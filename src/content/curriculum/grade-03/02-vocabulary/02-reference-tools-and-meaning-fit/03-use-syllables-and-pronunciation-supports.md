## Teaching Goal

Teach students that syllable and pronunciation supports help them read a word, while definitions explain meaning.

## Student Outcome

The student can use syllable breaks or simple pronunciation cues without confusing them with the word's meaning.

## Misconception Checks

Treating pronunciation as a definition; thinking a longer word must mean a harder idea; ignoring syllable breaks when decoding.

## Teaching Note

Show a friendly entry for `delicate` with syllable breaks and ask which part helps you say it and which helps you understand it.

## Questions

```question

  - left: pronunciation support
    right: say the word
  - left: example sentence
    right: see the word in use
explanation: Different entry parts help with meaning, reading, saying, or using a word.
hint: Ask whether the part helps meaning or word reading.
```
