## Teaching Goal

Students use je vais à... with familiar school and town destinations.

## Student Outcome

The student can understand and produce controlled sentences about going to school, the library, the park, the café, or the cinema.

## Misconception Checks

Treating je vais as complete without a destination; overgeneralizing à forms; confusing je vais and j'aime when both use familiar nouns.

## Teaching Note

Keep aller as a chunk. Use "je vais à + place" and do not teach the full paradigm.
