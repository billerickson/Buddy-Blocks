## Teaching Goal

Students choose tu or vous forms based on relationship and politeness in controlled exchanges.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify when an informal or polite address fits and choose a matching question line.

## Misconception Checks

Treating tu and vous as spelling variants; using tu with every person because it is shorter; thinking vous is only plural.

## Teaching Note

Do not overteach conjugation. Keep the focus on social meaning and a few memorized question forms.
