## Teaching Goal

Students add short reasons and connectors to preference statements.

## Student Outcome

The student can use parce que, et, and mais to make a simple preference statement with a reason.

## Misconception Checks

Writing a long English-style explanation; using parce que without a reason; choosing an adjective that contradicts the preference.

## Teaching Note

Model compact reasons: J'aime la musique parce que c'est amusant. Je n'aime pas les maths parce que c'est difficile.
