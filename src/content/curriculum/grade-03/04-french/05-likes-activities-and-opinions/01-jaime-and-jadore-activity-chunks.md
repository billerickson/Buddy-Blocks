## Teaching Goal

Students state positive preferences using j'aime and j'adore with common activity chunks.

## Student Outcome

The student can understand and produce short positive preference statements about familiar activities.

## Misconception Checks

Translating "I like to" word by word; changing the activity verb after j'aime; confusing activity nouns with action chunks.

## Teaching Note

Treat j'aime jouer as a chunk meaning "I like to play." Avoid a formal infinitive lecture.
