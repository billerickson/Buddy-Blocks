## Teaching Goal

Students identify common foods and drinks and request them politely.

## Student Outcome

The student can understand food words with articles and use je voudrais ___, s'il vous plaît in a controlled request.

## Misconception Checks

Dropping articles with food nouns; using je veux in every service situation; translating "I would like" word by word.

## Teaching Note

Model a café exchange and contrast a polite request with a too-direct one.

  - Je voudrais
