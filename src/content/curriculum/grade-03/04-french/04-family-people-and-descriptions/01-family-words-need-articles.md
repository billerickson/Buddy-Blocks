## Teaching Goal

Students learn family nouns with articles as part of the word, not optional extras.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify close family words and choose le, la, l', un, une, or les in controlled contexts.

## Misconception Checks

Dropping articles; assuming gender is always about the real person's gender rather than a noun pattern; confusing soeur and frère.

## Teaching Note

Teach words as chunks: le frère, la soeur, les parents. Say "the article travels with the noun."
