## Teaching Goal

Support students in handling unfamiliar Greek and Roman names, places, and character relationships through story context.

## Student Outcome

The student can use pronunciation cues and relationship clues to keep track of a small myth cast.

## Misconception Checks

Understanding a myth requires memorizing long lists; similar-looking names are interchangeable; place names are trivia with no story role.

## Teaching Note

Introduce only three or four names at once and say them aloud before reading the passage.

    right: God often linked with the sea
