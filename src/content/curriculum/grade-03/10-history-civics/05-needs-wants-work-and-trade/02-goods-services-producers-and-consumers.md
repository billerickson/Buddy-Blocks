## Teaching Goal

Teach what is exchanged and the roles people play in economic activity.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify goods, services, producers, and consumers in simple scenarios.

## Misconception Checks

Thinking services are not real because they are not objects; treating all workers as producers of goods only; thinking consumers must always buy with money.

## Teaching Note

Use a baker and a bus driver to contrast goods and services before adding producer/consumer roles.

## Questions
