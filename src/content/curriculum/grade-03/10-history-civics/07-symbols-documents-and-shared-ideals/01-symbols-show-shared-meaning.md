## Teaching Goal

Teach students to interpret civic symbols as sources of shared meaning.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify a symbol detail and connect it to a possible civic meaning.

## Misconception Checks

Treating symbol meaning as only the pictured object; assuming every viewer has exactly the same feeling; memorizing a symbol name without explaining meaning.

## Teaching Note

Use a school or community symbol before national examples so students see how symbols work generally.
