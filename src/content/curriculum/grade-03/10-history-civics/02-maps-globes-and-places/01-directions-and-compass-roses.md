## Teaching Goal

Teach students to use a compass rose to describe location and movement.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify cardinal and intermediate directions and follow a simple directional route.

## Misconception Checks

Treating north as the same as "up" wherever a person is standing; mixing east and west; skipping intermediate directions.

## Teaching Note

Use the classroom or a simple grid map with a compass rose before moving to imaginary community maps.
