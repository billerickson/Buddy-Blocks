## Teaching Goal

Help students distinguish major place categories and connect maps to routes, regions, environments, and movement.

## Student Outcome

The student can classify simple place labels and use map clues to describe movement or regional features.

## Misconception Checks

Treating city, state, country, continent, and ocean as interchangeable; thinking a map shows everything about a place; ignoring environment when explaining movement.

## Teaching Note

Use nesting language: a city can be in a state, a state in a country, and a country on a continent.

```question
  - The bridge is north of the village.

  - The river is not on the map.
  - The bridge is south of the village.

hint: Look for the map clue that explains the route.
```
