## Teaching Goal

Teach students that map symbols must be interpreted with a map key.

## Student Outcome

The student can match symbols to meanings and answer simple questions using a map key.

## Misconception Checks

Guessing a symbol's meaning from its appearance alone; ignoring the key; thinking symbols show real size or exact appearance.

## Teaching Note

Show the same simple symbol used with two different keys to prove the key controls meaning.

## Questions
