## Teaching Goal

Establish broad time language for events that already happened, are happening now, or may happen later.

## Student Outcome

The student can classify simple events as past, present, or future and use before/after language in a sentence.

## Misconception Checks

Treating "long ago" and "yesterday" as the same; thinking future events are guaranteed to happen; using present for any event being discussed now.

## Teaching Note

Start with a familiar routine: "I ate breakfast," "I am learning now," and "I will go home later." Then widen to community and historical examples.
