## Teaching Goal

Use simple question prompts to expand bare sentences with basic clarifying information.

## Student Outcome

The student can add who, what, when, or where information to make a sentence clearer.

## Misconception Checks

Expansion means adding random words; every sentence must answer every question; added details can contradict the original sentence.

## Teaching Note

Start with "The child ran." Add who, where, and when without changing the core meaning.

## Questions

```question
key: u03_l02_q01_complete_expansion
type: multi-blank-cloze
prompt: Complete the expanded sentence.
parts:
  - ""
  - " carried the books "
  - " "
  - .
blanks:
  - correctAnswer: Jada
    choices:
      - Jada
      - After lunch
      - To the library
  - correctAnswer: after lunch
    choices:
      - after lunch
      - to the library
      - with careful steps
  - correctAnswer: to the library
    choices:
      - to the library
      - after lunch
      - with two books
explanation: The sentence adds who, when, and where details.
hint: Each blank should make the sentence clearer and grammatical.
```

```question
key: u03_l02_q02_where_detail
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the detail that tells where.
sentenceBefore: The class met
sentenceAfter: to practice the song.
choices:
  - in the music room
  - after recess
  - with careful voices
  - because the concert was soon
correctAnswer: in the music room
explanation: "'In the music room' tells where the class met."
hint: A where detail tells the place.
```

```question
key: u03_l02_q03_best_expansion
type: multiple-choice
prompt: "Bare sentence: 'The student waited.' Which expansion is clearest?"
choices:
  - The student waited by the office after lunch.
  - The student waited somewhere for something.
  - The student waited and waited and waited and waited.
  - The student waited after lunch with no place named.
correctAnswer: The student waited by the office after lunch.
explanation: It adds useful where and when information without changing the meaning.
hint: Choose the expansion that adds clear information.
```

```question
key: u03_l02_q04_expand_sentence
type: multi-blank-cloze
prompt: Expand the sentence by adding who and where.
parts:
  - ""
  - " returned the book "
  - .
blanks:
  - correctAnswer: Mateo
    choices:
      - Mateo
      - After lunch
      - Carefully
  - correctAnswer: to the library
    choices:
      - to the library
      - after lunch
      - with a bookmark
explanation: A clear expansion adds who returned the book and where it went.
hint: The first blank should name a person, and the second should name a place.
```

```question
key: u03_l02_q05_question_word_to_detail
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each question word to the detail it asks for.
pairs:
  - left: who
    right: Milo
  - left: what
    right: returned the book
  - left: when
    right: after lunch
  - left: where
    right: to the library
explanation: Who, what, when, and where details help a reader picture the basic event.
hint: Ask each question about the sentence.
```

```question
key: u03_l02_q06_no_contradiction
type: multiple-choice
prompt: "Original sentence: 'The team practiced before school.' Which expansion keeps the meaning and adds where?"
choices:
  - The team practiced in the gym before school.
  - The team practiced after dinner before school.
  - The team never practiced before school.
  - The team practiced carefully before school.
correctAnswer: The team practiced in the gym before school.
explanation: It adds a place and keeps the original time.
hint: The expansion should not contradict the original sentence.
```
