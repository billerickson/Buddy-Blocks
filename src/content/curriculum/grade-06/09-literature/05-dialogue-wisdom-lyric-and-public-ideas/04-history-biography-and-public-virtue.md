## Teaching Goal

Teach students to read historical or biographical examples as shaped portraits that communicate public values.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify what virtue, warning, or civic idea a portrait emphasizes and how evidence creates that emphasis.

## Misconception Checks

Historical writing is only fact storage; biography automatically praises its subject; a public value is the same as a personal opinion.

## Questions

```question
key: cl4_u05_l04_q01_public_value
type: passage-question
prompt: Read the portrait and identify the public value emphasized.
passageTitle: The Judge's Bench
passage: |
  When the judge's son was accused, the judge left his high seat and stood among the citizens.
  "Today I am not father or magistrate," he said. "Let the law hear every voice before mine."
question: Which public value does the portrait most emphasize?
choices:
  - impartial justice
  - private revenge
  - love of luxury
  - fear of speaking
correctAnswer: impartial justice
explanation: The judge steps down from privilege and says the law should hear every voice before his.
hint: Look for how he treats his own position and his son's case.
```

```question
key: cl4_u05_l04_q02_action_value_match
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each detail to the value or idea it supports.
pairs:
  - left: left his high seat
    right: steps away from privilege
  - left: stood among the citizens
    right: places himself under public judgment
  - left: '"not father or magistrate"'
    right: refuses a private or official advantage
  - left: '"hear every voice before mine"'
    right: values fair hearing
explanation: A portrait communicates public value through selected actions and words.
hint: Ask what each action makes the reader admire or notice.
```

```question
key: cl4_u05_l04_q03_best_portrait_claim

  - The portrait presents justice as refusing personal advantage.
  - The portrait proves that judges should never have families.
  - The portrait says citizens should never speak.
  - The portrait praises the judge's high seat as the main hero.

```
