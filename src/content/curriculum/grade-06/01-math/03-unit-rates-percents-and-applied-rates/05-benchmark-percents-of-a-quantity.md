## Teaching Goal

Use benchmark percents to find amounts mentally or with simple steps.

## Student Outcome

The student can find 10%, 25%, 50%, 75%, and 100% of familiar quantities and explain the benchmark strategy.

## Key Ideas

Benchmark percents connect to fractions: 50% is half, 25% is one fourth, and 10% is one tenth.

## Misconception Checks

Multiply by the percent number directly; treat 25% as 25 times the quantity; ignore whether the result is reasonable.

## Teaching Note

Put 50%, 25%, and 10% on a percent bar and connect each to a fraction of the whole.

  - Find 50% of 40 = 20
  - Find 25% of 40 = 10
