## Teaching Goal

Introduce percent as a rate per 100 and connect it to fractions and decimals.

## Student Outcome

The student can interpret and convert common percents using per-100 reasoning.

## Key Ideas

35% means 35 per 100, or 35/100, or 0.35.

## Misconception Checks

Percent is always a whole-number count; percent values cannot connect to fractions or decimals; the percent sign changes the quantity without context.

## Teaching Note

Use a 100-square grid or percent bar to show 35 out of 100.
