## Teaching Goal

Apply unit rates to compare prices fairly.

## Student Outcome

The student can compute cost per item or per ounce and decide which option is the better buy.

## Key Ideas

Unit price compares costs for the same amount.

## Misconception Checks

Lowest total price is always best; largest package is always best; units can be ignored.

## Teaching Note

Compare two snack bags with different sizes and prices, then normalize both to one ounce.
