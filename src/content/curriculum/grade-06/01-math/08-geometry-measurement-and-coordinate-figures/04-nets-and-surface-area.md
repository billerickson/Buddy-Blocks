## Teaching Goal

Use nets to find surface area of rectangular prisms.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify all faces in a net, find each face area, and total them.

## Key Ideas

Surface area is the sum of the areas of every face.

## Misconception Checks

Count only visible faces; confuse surface area with volume; double the wrong faces.

## Teaching Note

Unfold a box into a net and label matching opposite faces.

