## Teaching Goal

Extend rectangular prism volume to fractional edge lengths.

## Student Outcome

The student can compute volume as length x width x height and interpret cubic units.

## Key Ideas

Volume counts how many unit cubes or fractional unit cubes fill a prism.

## Misconception Checks

Fractional dimensions cannot produce volume; use surface area instead of volume; omit cubic units.

## Teaching Note

Use a prism with dimensions `1/2`, `4`, and `3` to show half-size layers.

## Questions

```question
key: u08_l03_q01_fraction_volume
type: text-input

  - 6 cubic units
answerType: text

hint: Multiply the three edge lengths.
```
