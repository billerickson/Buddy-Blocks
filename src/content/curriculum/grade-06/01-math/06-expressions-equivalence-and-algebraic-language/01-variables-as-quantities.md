## Teaching Goal

Establish variables as numbers that are unknown or changing, not labels.

## Student Outcome

The student can interpret a variable and a coefficient-like expression in context.

## Key Ideas

A letter can stand for a quantity; `5m` means five groups of the quantity `m`.

## Misconception Checks

A variable names an object; `5m` means five meters in all contexts; variables are always one fixed mystery number.

## Teaching Note

Compare "5 meters" with `5m` where `m` is the number of minutes or muffins.

## Questions
