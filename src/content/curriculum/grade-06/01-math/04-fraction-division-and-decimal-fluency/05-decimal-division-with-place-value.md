## Teaching Goal

Divide decimals using place-value reasoning and estimation.

## Student Outcome

The student can compute decimal quotients and explain why the decimal point belongs where it does.

## Key Ideas

Decimal division can be checked by estimating the quotient's size and tracking decimal place values.

## Misconception Checks

Move decimal points by memorized pattern only; produce a quotient far outside the estimate; ignore trailing zeros.

## Teaching Note

Estimate first: `12.6 divided by 3` should be a little more than 4, not 0.42 or 42.

## Questions

  - About 10
