## Teaching Goal

Help students revise usage and tone for audience while respecting language variation.

## Student Outcome

The student can choose a formal, informal, or academic register that fits a situation without judging other contexts as inferior.

## Key Ideas

Register is language fit for audience and purpose; standard academic English is useful in formal school contexts but not a measure of a person's worth or intelligence.

## Misconception Checks

Formal writing means stiff writing; informal or dialect language is bad grammar; changing register means making writing sound fancy.
