    passage: A class made a study page for Author's Craft. The word "tone" means the writer's attitude toward the subject. The word "mood" means the feeling a text creates for the reader. The word "metaphor" means a comparison that says one thing is another. The word "simile" means a comparison using like or as. The word "personification" means giving human traits to something nonhuman. The word "irony" means a contrast between what is expected and what happens. The word "point of view" means the perspective from which a story is told. The word "voice" means the author's or narrator's style and personality. The word "diction" means the author's word choice. After reading the page, they practiced choosing meanings before typing any words from memory.

    explanation: The passage uses "tone" for the writer's attitude toward the subject.
