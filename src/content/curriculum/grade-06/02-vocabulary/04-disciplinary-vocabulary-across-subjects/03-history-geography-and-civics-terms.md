## Teaching Goal

Interpret social studies vocabulary through source, map, and civic contexts.

## Student Outcome

The student can use words such as source, region, culture, economy, citizen, and perspective accurately.

## Misconception Checks

Culture means only food or clothing; economy means money only; perspective means opinion with no evidence.

## Teaching Note

Use a map caption and a source note to show how the same topic can include region, culture, economy, and perspective.

## Questions

  - the exact distance between two towns
  - a law passed by a court
