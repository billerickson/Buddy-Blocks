## Teaching Goal

Build vocabulary for observing, classifying, testing variables, reading data, and drawing conclusions.

## Student Outcome

The student can interpret science process words in short experiment or data contexts.

## Misconception Checks

Observe means guess; data is the same as conclusion; variable means any detail in a story.

## Teaching Note

Show a tiny investigation with observation, variable, data, and conclusion labeled.

## Questions

```question
key: u04_l02_q01_science_term_match
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each science term to its role.
pairs:
  - left: observe
    right: notice using senses or tools
  - left: classify
    right: sort by shared traits
  - left: data
    right: recorded information or measurements
  - left: conclusion
    right: an answer based on evidence
explanation: Science vocabulary often names actions in an investigation.
hint: Think about what a scientist does before and after collecting evidence.
```

```question
key: u04_l02_q02_observe_not_infer
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence is an observation, not an inference?
choices:
  - The leaf has brown spots.
  - The plant is probably sick.
  - The soil must be too dry.
  - The plant will die tomorrow.
correctAnswer: The leaf has brown spots.
explanation: An observation states what is noticed. The other choices interpret or predict.
hint: Choose what someone can directly see.
```

```question
key: u04_l02_q03_variable_context
type: passage-question
prompt: Read the investigation note.
passageTitle: Plant Test
passage: |
  Two plants were the same size. One plant received sunlight, and the other stayed in a dark cabinet. Both plants received the same amount of water.
question: In this investigation, which variable is being changed?
choices:
  - amount of sunlight
  - amount of water
  - starting plant size
  - the word cabinet
correctAnswer: amount of sunlight
explanation: The sunlight changes between the two plants, while water and starting size stay the same.
hint: Find what is different between the two plants.
```


```question
key: u04_l02_q06_evidence_conclusion

  - The ice on the desk melted faster.
```
