## Teaching Goal

Teach students to find the term that connects the two premises in a syllogism.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify the middle term and distinguish it from the two terms in the conclusion.

## Misconception Checks

Picking the term that appears in the conclusion; assuming the longest phrase is the middle term; missing repeated multi-word terms.

## Questions

```question
key: u03_l02_q01_middle_term_basic
type: multiple-choice
prompt: "Find the middle term: All robins are birds. All birds are animals. Therefore all robins are animals."
choices:
  - birds
  - robins
  - animals
  - all
correctAnswer: birds
explanation: Birds appears in both premises and connects robins to animals.
hint: Look for the term that appears in the premises but not in the conclusion.
```
