## Teaching Goal

Teach the two-part test for independent clauses: subject plus verb and complete thought.

## Student Outcome

The student can decide whether a word group is an independent clause.

## Key Ideas

An independent clause has a subject, has a verb, and can stand alone as a complete thought.

## Misconception Checks

Treating any subject-verb group as complete; judging by length; relying on capital letters and periods instead of meaning.
