## Teaching Goal

Teach definitions as boundary tools rather than official-sounding phrases.

## Student Outcome

The student can choose a clear definition that helps decide what belongs in a category.

## Key Ideas

A clear definition gives the important features of a thing, not just a favorite example or vague description.

## Misconception Checks

Thinking a definition is clear because it sounds fancy; giving only an example; defining a word with the same word.
