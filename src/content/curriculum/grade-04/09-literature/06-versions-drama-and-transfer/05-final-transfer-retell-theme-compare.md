## Teaching Goal

Consolidate Level 2 skills through a new adapted tale that requires retelling, theme support, and comparison.

## Student Outcome

The student can retell a new tale, choose a supported theme, identify a pattern, and compare it to a known story focus.

## Misconception Checks

Relying on memory of familiar stories instead of reading the new text; giving a theme without evidence; comparing without a focus.

## Questions

  - Rhea carries a lamp to the hill shrine.
