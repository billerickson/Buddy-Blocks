## Teaching Goal

Use commas to connect simple speaker tags with quoted words.

## Student Outcome

The student can punctuate dialogue when the speaker tag comes before or after the quotation.

## Key Ideas

A speaker tag tells who spoke; a comma often separates the tag from the exact words in simple dialogue.

## Misconception Checks

The comma belongs outside all quotation marks; the speaker tag is part of the exact words; the comma can be skipped because quotation marks are enough.

## Questions

```question
key: u05_l03_q01_tag_before_comma
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence uses the comma correctly before quoted words?
choices:
  - Lena said, "Wait here."
  - Lena said "Wait here."
  - Lena, said "Wait here."
  - Lena said "Wait, here."
correctAnswer: Lena said, "Wait here."
explanation: When the speaker tag comes before the quotation, a comma separates the tag from the exact words.
hint: Look between said and the opening quotation mark.
```

```question
key: u05_l03_q02_tag_after_comma
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence uses the comma correctly when the tag comes after the quoted words?
choices:
  - '"Wait here," Lena said.'
  - '"Wait here" Lena said.'
  - '"Wait here." Lena said.'
  - '"Wait, here" Lena said.'
correctAnswer: '"Wait here," Lena said.'
explanation: For a statement with the tag after it, the comma goes inside the closing quotation mark.
hint: The comma stays with the quoted words before the closing quotation mark.
```

```question
key: u05_l03_q03_repair_tag_before
type: error-correction
prompt: Correct the dialogue comma.
sentence: Lena said "Wait here."
acceptedAnswers:
  - Lena said, "Wait here."
explanation: A comma belongs after the speaker tag Lena said.
hint: Add the comma between the tag and the opening quotation mark.
```

```question
key: u05_l03_q04_order_dialogue_parts
type: order-items
prompt: Put the parts in order to make correctly punctuated dialogue.
items:
  - '"I am ready."'
  - said,
  - Milo
correctOrder:
  - Milo
  - said,
  - '"I am ready."'
explanation: The speaker tag Milo said, comes before the quoted words.
hint: Start with the speaker, then the tag comma, then the exact words.
```

```question

  - Omar called
  - The bus is here

explanation: Omar called tells who spoke.
hint: The speaker tag is outside the quotation marks.
```

```question
key: u05_l03_q06_choose_dialogue_comma
type: fill-blank

sentenceBefore: Rina asked
sentenceAfter: ' "May I join?"'
choices:
  - ","
  - .
  - "?"
  - ":"
correctAnswer: ","
explanation: A comma separates the speaker tag Rina asked from the quoted question.
hint: The question mark belongs inside the quoted words, not after the tag.
```
