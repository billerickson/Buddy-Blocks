## Teaching Goal

Place quotation marks around only the exact words a speaker says.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify quoted words and choose correct quotation mark placement in simple dialogue.

## Key Ideas

Quotation marks hug the speaker's exact words, not the speaker tag.

## Misconception Checks

Quotation marks go around the whole sentence; quotation marks go only around the speaker's name; any sentence with said needs quotation marks around every word.

## Questions

```question
key: u05_l02_q02_correct_quote_placement
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence places quotation marks correctly?
choices:
  - Maya said, "I found the key."
  - '"Maya said, I found the key."'
  - Maya "said, I found the key."
  - Maya said, I "found the key."
correctAnswer: Maya said, "I found the key."
explanation: Only I found the key. is inside quotation marks because those are Maya's exact words.
hint: Quotation marks should hug only the spoken words.
```
