## Teaching Goal

Combine capitalization, end punctuation, quotation marks, and simple speaker tags in dialogue.

## Student Outcome

The student can correct a focused dialogue sentence with the first word of a quotation capitalized and end punctuation inside the quotation marks.

## Key Ideas

In simple dialogue, the spoken sentence begins with a capital letter and its end punctuation stays inside the closing quotation mark.

## Misconception Checks

End punctuation belongs outside the quotation marks; the first word of quoted speech can stay lowercase; punctuation can be fixed without checking the exact words.

## Questions

explanation: The quoted question begins with a capital letter, and the question mark stays inside the quotation marks.
hint: Check the first word of the quoted sentence and the end punctuation.
