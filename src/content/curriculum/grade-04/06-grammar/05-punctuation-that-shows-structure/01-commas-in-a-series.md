## Teaching Goal

Use commas to separate three or more items in a series.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify a series and place commas between the items.

## Key Ideas

A series is a list of three or more items; commas help readers see the items separately.

## Misconception Checks

Any pause needs a comma; two items need a series comma; commas can go after every word in a sentence.

## Questions

```question
key: u05_l01_q01_identify_series
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence has a series?
choices:
  - We packed socks, maps, snacks, and water.
  - We packed socks and maps.
  - We packed after lunch.
  - We packed the blue bag.
correctAnswer: We packed socks, maps, snacks, and water.
explanation: Socks, maps, snacks, and water is a list of three or more items.
hint: A series has three or more items.
```

```question
key: u05_l01_q02_add_series_commas
type: error-correction
prompt: Add commas to the series.
sentence: We packed socks maps snacks and water.
acceptedAnswers:
  - We packed socks, maps, snacks, and water.
  - We packed socks, maps, snacks and water.
explanation: Commas separate the list items so readers can see each thing packed.
hint: Separate the items in the list.
```

```question
key: u05_l01_q03_choose_correct_commas
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence uses commas correctly?
choices:
  - The trail was rocky, steep, and narrow.
  - The trail was, rocky steep and narrow.
  - The trail was rocky steep, and narrow.
  - The trail, was rocky, steep and, narrow.
correctAnswer: The trail was rocky, steep, and narrow.
explanation: Rocky, steep, and narrow are three describing words in a series.
hint: Put commas between the three items, not after random pauses.
```

```question
key: u05_l01_q04_two_items_no_series_comma
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence does not need a comma for a series?
choices:
  - Nia brought pencils and paper.
  - Nia brought pencils, paper, and tape.
  - Nia brought red, blue, and green folders.
  - Nia brought rulers, markers, and glue.
correctAnswer: Nia brought pencils and paper.
explanation: Pencils and paper is a list of two items, not a series of three or more.
hint: Count the items in the list.
```

```question

  - "5"
```
