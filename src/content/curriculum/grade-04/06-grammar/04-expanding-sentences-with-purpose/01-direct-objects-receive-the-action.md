## Teaching Goal

Identify direct objects after action verbs in short sentences.

## Student Outcome

The student can ask whom or what after an action verb to find the direct object.

## Key Ideas

A direct object receives the action of an action verb.

## Misconception Checks

Any word after a verb is a direct object; linking verbs have direct objects; prepositional phrase objects are direct objects.

## Questions

```question
key: u04_l01_q01_find_direct_object
type: multiple-choice
prompt: What is the direct object in this sentence? Ava carried the lantern.
choices:
  - Ava
  - carried
  - the lantern
  - the
correctAnswer: the lantern
explanation: Ask carried what? Ava carried the lantern. The lantern receives the action.
hint: Find the action verb, then ask whom or what.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q02_question_frame
type: fill-blank
prompt: Complete the question that finds the direct object in Nora opened the window.
sentenceBefore: "Nora opened "
sentenceAfter: "?"
choices:
  - what
  - where
  - when
  - how
correctAnswer: what
explanation: Opened what? The window. That question finds the direct object.
hint: Direct objects answer whom or what after an action verb.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q03_linking_no_object
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence has no direct object because the verb is linking?
choices:
  - The soup tasted salty.
  - The chef tasted the soup.
  - Mina kicked the ball.
  - Dad washed the mugs.
correctAnswer: The soup tasted salty.
explanation: Tasted links soup to salty in this sentence. Salty describes the subject; it does not receive an action.
hint: Look for the sentence where the word after the verb describes the subject.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q04_action_to_object
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each action verb to a direct object that can receive the action.
pairs:
  - left: read
    right: the chapter
  - left: fixed
    right: the bike
  - left: drew
    right: a map
  - left: washed
    right: the cups
explanation: Each direct object answers what after the action verb.
hint: Ask read what, fixed what, drew what, and washed what.
```

```question
key: u04_l01_q05_phrase_not_object
type: passage-question
prompt: Read the sentence and answer the question.
passageTitle: Object Or Phrase
passage: After lunch, Maya kicked the ball across the yard.
question: What is the direct object?
choices:
  - lunch
  - Maya
  - the ball
  - the yard
correctAnswer: the ball
explanation: Kicked what? The ball. Across the yard tells where the ball went.
hint: Ask what received the action kicked.
```
