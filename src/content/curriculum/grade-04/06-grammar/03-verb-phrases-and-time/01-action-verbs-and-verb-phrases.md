## Teaching Goal

Identify action verbs and simple verb phrases in complete sentences.

## Student Outcome

The student can find the word or words that tell what the subject does.

## Key Ideas

A verb phrase can be one action verb or a main verb with helpers.

## Misconception Checks

Only physical actions count as verbs; the last word in the sentence is the verb; a verb phrase cannot have more than one word.

## Questions

```question
key: u03_l01_q01_find_verb_phrase
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which words are the verb phrase in this sentence? The dog will chase the ball.
choices:
  - The dog
  - will chase
  - the ball
  - chase the ball
correctAnswer: will chase
explanation: Will helps the main verb chase, so will chase is the verb phrase.
hint: Look for the helper plus the action word.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q02_choose_action
type: fill-blank
prompt: Choose the action verb that completes the sentence.
sentenceBefore: "The artist "
sentenceAfter: " a mural on the wall."
choices:
  - painted
  - blue
  - under
  - quiet
correctAnswer: painted
explanation: Painted tells what the artist did.
hint: Choose the word that shows an action.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q03_subject_action_match
type: match-pairs
prompt: Match each subject to a fitting action verb.
pairs:
  - left: The bell
    right: rang
  - left: The swimmer
    right: splashed
  - left: The chef
    right: stirred
  - left: The baby
    right: crawled
explanation: Each action verb tells what the subject did.
hint: Picture what each subject can do.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q04_mental_action
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which word is the action verb in this sentence? I remembered the answer.
choices:
  - I
  - remembered
  - the
  - answer
correctAnswer: remembered
explanation: Remembered is a verb even though the action happens in the mind.
hint: Some actions are thinking actions.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q05_type_verb_phrase
type: text-input
prompt: "Type the verb phrase in this sentence: The team is practicing after school."
acceptedAnswers:
  - is practicing
answerType: text
explanation: Is helps the main verb practicing, so the full verb phrase is is practicing.
hint: Include both the helper and the main verb.
```

```question
key: u03_l01_q06_two_word_verb_phrase
type: multiple-choice
prompt: Which sentence has a two-word verb phrase?
choices:
  - The ducks swim across the pond.
  - The ducks are swimming across the pond.
  - The ducks near the pond.
  - The ducks with yellow feet.
correctAnswer: The ducks are swimming across the pond.
explanation: Are swimming is a two-word verb phrase.
hint: Look for a helping verb before the main verb.
```
