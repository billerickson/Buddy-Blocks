## Teaching Goal

Interpret scientific reflection as a cross-disciplinary argument about life, models, and limits.

## Student Outcome

The student can distinguish observation, model, analogy, and philosophical question in a scientific-humanistic passage.

## Misconception Checks

Scientific reflection is only fact reporting; humanities questions are irrelevant to science; analogy is proof by itself.

## Questions
