## Teaching Goal

Show how early community texts reason through belief, practice, conflict, and counsel.

## Student Outcome

The student can identify audience, issue, claim, reason, and counsel in an acts or epistle-style passage.

## Misconception Checks

Epistles are private notes without argument; counsel is merely command; audience does not matter.

  - A community claims freedom but neglects mutual service.
