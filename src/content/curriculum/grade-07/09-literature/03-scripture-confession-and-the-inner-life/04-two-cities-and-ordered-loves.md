## Teaching Goal

Connect inner life, love, community, and historical imagination.

## Student Outcome

The student can explain the idea that communities are shaped by what they love and order most.

## Misconception Checks

The two cities are only geographic places; love is only emotion; private inward life has no civic consequence.
