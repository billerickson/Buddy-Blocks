## Teaching Goal

Read political advice through problem, audience, fortune, necessity, and consequence.

## Student Outcome

The student can distinguish analysis of power from simple moral endorsement or dismissal.

## Misconception Checks

Machiavelli's advice is either pure villainy or plain common sense; audience and situation do not matter.
